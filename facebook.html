Word Smashing

Word smashing is a free online word search puzzle.

Instructions:
Make words! lots of words! two at once or heaps in a row for bonus points!
Get words up, down, forward and backwards!
On easy, get words with 2+ letters, on medium 3+, Hard 4+!
To move, click a letter and click a space!
You can move if there's a clear path!
Each turn 3 more letters appear, when the board fills up its game over!
Looking for a harder challenge? Play Word Smashing Time Trials.
Some people & place names don't count!
