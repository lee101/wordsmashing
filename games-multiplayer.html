Word Smashing Multiplayer

Word smashing is a free online multiplayer word search puzzle.

Instructions:
You are red! You can move your letters and make words with anyones letters!
Get words up, down, forward and backwards!
On easy, get words with 2+ letters, on medium 3+, Hard 4+!
To move, click a letter and click a space!
You can move if theres a clear path!
Each turn 3 more letters appear, when the board fills up its game over!
More Pressure? Play Word Smashing Time Trials.
Less Pressure? Play Word Smashing Classic.
Some people & place names don't count!
